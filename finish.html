Let’s Bake! You will need: o One 9 inch cake pan (or three 8 inch springform pans if making keto version) o 3 T oil or butter to grease the pan. o Parchment paper (optional) o Patience, perseverance INGREDIENTS: FLOUR MIX: 2 2/3 c. flour + 1-3 T leavening + ½ tsp salt SWEETENER: 1 cup evaporated cane sugar or allulose. FLAVORINGS: 1 T Vanilla “EGG” MIX: 3 eggs or ¾ c. applesauce. “DAIRY” MIX: 1 cup whole milk or hazelnut milk, 2 T apple cider vinegar. SHORTENING: 1 cup butter or vegetable spread. WHIPPED TOPPING/FROSTING: 1 cup whipping cream or 2 cans chilled coconut, 2 T sugar*, 1/2 tsp Vanilla*. (*optional) DECORATIONS: ½ cup sliced strawberries or raspberries. Instructions (Quantities depend on your ingredient list) 1. Preheat oven to 350°F 2. Butter or oil a 9 inch cake pan (line with parchment paper if available) 3. Measure out ¾ to 1 cup of milk. Add 2 T. of 4. Sift together 2 2/3 cups of flour mixture, 1-3 T of leavening, ½ teaspoon sea salt into a bowl and set aside. 5. Whip eggs or prepare egg alternatives and add flavoring. Set aside 6. Whip butter and sweetener, and add “egg mix” to bowl 7. Slowly add Flour Mix, whipping batter intermittently. 8. Pour batter into pan. 9. Bake at 350°F for 60 min (default recipe) to 70 min (Gluten-free recipe). 10. Cake will be firm on surface when baked, and toothpick will come out clean. 11. Remove from oven, cover with a tea towel and let cool for 15 to 20 minutes before inverting cake onto serving plate. 12. Continue to cool until no longer warm to touch (1-2 hours). 13. Prepare whipped topping: Pour heavy cream or chilled coconut cream into mixer. Add optional 2 T sweetener (granulated or powdered is best for thicker topping). 14. Spread whipped topping on top and sides of cake. 15. Decorate as desired. 16. ENJOY! CAKE FOR EVERYONE Serving Size: 10 Time to Prepare: 20 min. Time to Cook: 60 to 70 min.
